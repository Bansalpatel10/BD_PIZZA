About Us

We've beem making healthy food last for 10 years

The story of India's fastest growing pizza place started back in 2013 when Bansal Manavadariya and Daksh Mangukiya opened his very first pizzeria in his hometown Surat. Inspired by his own search for delicious pizza options, he started to wonder if there was a better way of doing pizza – and business. The last thing the world needed was another pizzeria, but maybe this one could be different and everyone could get exactly what they wanted, made fresh on demand. It all started sounding pretty great, and soon Both was opening the first BD Pizza in his hometown Surat and there was no looking back. As of today, BD Pizza has 100+ outlets across India.
